Our Story

Founded in 1985 by marine enthusiast Captain Jack Marlow, WaveRider Outboards began as a small repair shop on the shores of Lake Michigan. What started as a passion project quickly grew into the region's most trusted outboard motor specialist.

After serving in the Navy as a marine engineer, Captain Jack recognized the need for honest, knowledgeable outboard motor services in the Great Lakes area. His commitment to quality workmanship and customer education became the foundation of our business.

Today, WaveRider Outboards is still family-owned and operated, now with second-generation owner Sarah Marlow at the helm. We've expanded to three locations across Michigan and Wisconsin, but we've never lost our personal touch or passion for marine excellence.
